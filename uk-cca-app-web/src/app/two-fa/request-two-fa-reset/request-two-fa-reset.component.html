Request two-factor authentication reset

If you have lost your device and cannot access your authenticator, you must request a reset from your Regulator or Advanced User.

User

Contact your Advanced User

Regulator

Contact your Regulator Administrator

You can contact also the CCA Helpdesk. If you are sending an email, use the subject line 'I have lost access to my authenticator app - Climate Change Agreements Scheme', and include the name of the account you belong to.
