Privacy Notice

Privacy notice: how we use your personal information

The Environment Agency is the controller for the personal data we process for the Climate Change Agreements (CCA) Register service under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and Data Protection Act 2018 (DPA 2018). Our contact details are available on GOV.UK

Our personal information charter explains:

  • how to contact our Data Protection Officer
  • what we do with your personal information (personal data) in general
  • your rights and how to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office.

The personal data we need

The personal data we collect about you includes:

  • full name
  • address
  • contact details (email, telephone and fax)
  • employer name
  • job title or position
  • information on how you use the CCA register, including cookies

We collect these details from:

  • the CCA contact person for the sector association
  • the Responsible Person and the Administrative Contact for each target unit
  • the contact person for each CCA facility
  • representatives of an operator who want to access the CCA register
  • agents acting on behalf of operators or sector associations who want to access the CCA register
  • our staff and our contractor's staff who want to access the CCA register

We are allowed to process your personal data because we have official authority as the environmental regulator. The lawful basis for processing your personal data is to perform a task in the public interest that is set out in law as administrator under the Climate Change Agreements (Administration) Regulations 2012 (as amended).

If you don't provide all the information requested then we may not be able to enter into a CCA. If you do not maintain the information then your organisation may be liable for a penalty and/or its agreement may be terminated.

What we do with your personal data

We use your personal data to process your (or your client's) application for a CCA and to maintain your (or your client's) CCA.

We do not use your personal data to make an automated decision or for automated profiling.

We will share your personal data with:

  • our contractors who act as our agents for administering the scheme
  • credit reference or fraud prevention agencies
  • our internet security providers
  • the IT suppliers who run the CCA register

How long we keep your personal data

We will keep your personal data for the duration of your organisation’s involvement in the CCA scheme plus an additional 4 years.

Where your personal data is processed and stored

We store and process your personal data on our data processors Microsoft and Amazon Web Services, it may be transferred and stored securely outside the UK and EEA where it will be subject to equivalent legal protection through the use of Model Contract Clauses.
