Certification status

The facility’s certification status determines whether an operator can claim the Climate Change Levy (CCL) relief for a specific certification period.

  • Certified facility status means that the operator will be able to claim CCL relief for the certification, and the start date you enter will be shown on the Reduced Rate Certificate for this facility
  • Decertified facility status means that the facility will not appear on the Reduced Rate Certificate, and the operator will not be able to claim CCL relief for the certification period
