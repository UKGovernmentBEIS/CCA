Provide information on the activities carried out and the energy used at the facility.

To determine the improvement target for each facility included in an agreement, we need to know:

  • the boundary which encloses the land used in connection with the operation of the installation
  • the different categories of activities carried out on the site that consume energy, i.e.:
    • eligible
    • directly associated
    • ineligible
  • how much of the energy used on site can be included in the eligible facility (the 70% rule)
  • whether the eligible facility includes plant carrying out an activity listed in The Greenhouse Gas Emissions Trading Scheme Order 2020

The sections of the form below require you to upload the evidence we need to understand the extent of the facility.

The evidence you provide should be consistent between each of the relevant sections.
