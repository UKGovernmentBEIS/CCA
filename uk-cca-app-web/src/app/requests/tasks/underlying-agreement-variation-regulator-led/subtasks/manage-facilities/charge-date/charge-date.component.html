Do you want to change the subsistence charge start date from the default?

By default, this facility will become eligible for a subsistence charge fee as soon as their assent to the underlying agreement is uploaded by the administrator. If the facility has already paid for the current year whilst it was part of a previous target unit, you may need to override this default date.

Please note that the subsistence charge period starts on 1 January each year.
